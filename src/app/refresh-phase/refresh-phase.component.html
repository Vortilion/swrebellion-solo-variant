Refresh Phase

Retrieve Leaders Each player takes all of his leaders from the game board and returns them to his leader pool. If a leader is still on a mission card, it returns to the leader pool, and the card returns to the player’s hand Draw Missions Rebel player draws two mission cards and, if there are more than 10 cards in his hand, he discards down to 10 cards The Empire does not draw Mission cards during the Refresh Phase.
